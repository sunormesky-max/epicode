# Code of Conduct

Epicode is an open-source project. We want the community to be respectful, welcoming, and safe for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on the work, not the person.
- Give feedback in good faith.
- Respect differing opinions and experiences.
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started.
- Use inclusive language and be mindful of different cultural backgrounds.
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Personal attacks, insults, or public shaming
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or contributions
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission
- Trolling, spamming, or sustained off-topic discussions
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting Process

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it promptly. Reports are taken seriously and will be handled with discretion.

### How to Report

You can report incidents through any of the following channels:

1. **GitHub Issues**: For public, non-sensitive reports that can be discussed openly, open an issue in the repository.

2. **GitHub Security Advisories**: For sensitive matters that should not be discussed publicly, use the [Security Advisories](https://github.com/sunorme/sunormesky-max_epicode/security/advisories) feature.

3. **Email**: Contact the maintainers directly at `sunorme@163.com`. This is the preferred channel for private or sensitive reports.

### What to Include

When submitting a report, please include:
- Your contact information (so we can follow up with you)
- A description of what happened, including dates and times
- Where it happened (issue thread, PR, discussion, etc.)
- Who was involved
- Any relevant screenshots, links, or context
- Whether you believe the situation is ongoing

### Response Process

1. **Acknowledgment**: You will receive an acknowledgment within 48 hours of submitting a report.
2. **Review**: The maintainers will review the report and may contact you for additional information.
3. **Resolution**: The maintainers will determine appropriate action based on the [governance enforcement process](.github/GOVERNANCE.md#code-of-conduct-enforcement).
4. **Follow-up**: You will be informed of the outcome when the process is complete, subject to privacy constraints.

### Confidentiality

All reports are handled confidentially. We will not share your identity without your consent, except when required by law or when necessary to protect the safety of community members.

### No Retaliation

Retaliation against anyone who reports a concern in good faith is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate removal from the project.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces, including:
- GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Project documentation and wikis
- Community chat channels and forums
- Project events and meetups (virtual or in-person)
- One-on-one communications related to the project
- Social media interactions when representing the project

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this code of conduct. See the [Governance document](.github/GOVERNANCE.md#code-of-conduct-enforcement) for details on the enforcement process, including investigation, resolution, and appeals.
